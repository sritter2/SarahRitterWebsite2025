American Sign Language


How to Learn American Sign Language:

  1. Take Classes
    • Take Online Classes
    • Find a Private Tutor
    • Take Community Classes
  2. Practice Makes Perfect
    • Always find excuses to fingerspell
    • Think of new vocab words to learn when you're out and about
  3. Communicate with Deaf People
    • Attend deaf churches
    • Join local facebook groups
  4. Ask for feedback
    • Deaf People are blunt, but don't be afraid to ask for feedback. I'ts helpful!
